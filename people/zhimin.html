Research

I am a psychology graduate student at UC Berkeley. I study the neural mechanisms that enable us to perceive and interact with the world in everyday life. In particular, I am interested in how people maintain a coherent and stable perception of the environment and their complex abilities to deal with emotional content in social interaction. To learn more, please visit my webpage.
